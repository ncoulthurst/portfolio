Our Story

From a small trading company in 1898 to a leading European group. This is the timeline of how we got here.

1898

The Very Beginning

At the end of the nineteenth century, the founder of our company, Theodoor Simon Eskens, started a trading company for raw materials in industry at 5 Thorbeckeplein in Amsterdam.

1981

Expansion and Move

Martien Seijsener, who joined Eskens in 1975, takes over the trading company. He introduces the company to the 20th century with expansion across national borders. The company moves to Ede.

1989

Sales & Service

Eskens starts its own service department. From a pure sales organization, the company grows into a total solution supplier. The first in-house technicians are employed.

1995

Entering the Tinting World

Eskens obtains the agency for color mixing machines from COROB. This is the first step toward the specialized division that will later be called ‘Eskens Tinting Solutions’.

2006

A New Generation

All Eskens shares are transferred from Martien Seijsener to Bas Seijsener and Joost Poot. They establish Eskens Industrial Cleaning.

2014

From Family Business to Group

The Eskens Group is founded, splitting Eskens Benelux into Eskens Tinting Solutions B.V. and Eskens Process Solutions B.V.

2018

100% Ownership of Stort Eskens

After years of a joint venture, Eskens Tinting Solutions acquires the remaining shares of Stort Eskens. The entity continues as Eskens Tinting Solutions UK Ltd.

2021

Central S.A.T. Rebrand

Central S.A.T. changes its name and continues as Eskens Tinting Solutions France. The office moves to brand new premises in Mandelieu La Napoule.
